ABOUT US

In 2023, there was a small, tight-knit group of ten friends who shared a passion for cycling. They were all passionate about the sport and committed to making the world a better place through the power of bicycling.

The ten friends decided to come together to form a bicycle website, with the mission of helping others discover the joy and freedom that comes with riding a bicycle. The website would also provide a platform for cyclists to explore new trails, share stories, and exchange bike knowledge.
The website grew quickly, becoming increasingly popular with cyclists all over the world. The group of ten friends soon found themselves working hard to keep the website up and running, and to keep up with the ever-expanding demand.

Soon, the friends had a thriving website, with more content and features than they initially imagined they could ever have. They had created something that was helping people all over the world find joy in cycling, and they were proud of their accomplishment.

But the website wasn't just a success for the ten friends. It was also a success story for the cycling community. As the website grew in popularity, more people were encouraged to take up cycling and to make it part of their life. The website had become a platform for cyclists to come together and share their stories, and it had become a place of community and celebration of cycling.
